ABOUT DRINKR

Drinkr is a site that allows users to post their favorite drinks and have other people comment on them. Our goal is to provide a way for drink enthusiasts to share their special orders with each other.

    HELP AND DOCUMENTATION:

  • Start by clicking the "Add a New Drink" button. Feel free to add a drink, and hit "Submit Drink" to add your drink, or "Cancel" to make a different one.
  • Check out some of the other drinks posted by people, you'll be surprised by what you find. People are quite creative!
  • You can click the arrows on the right side of each post to up vote or down vote a user's drink. An up vote means you liked their drink, and a down vote means you did not like their drink.
  • You can add or view comments, to see what other people thought of the drinks. Have fun and enjoy discussing drinks in a more creative way.
